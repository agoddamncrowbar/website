USIU Chess team wins big at Mombasa tournament!

The school chess team was recently hosted at the Technical University of Mombasa for an intercounty chess tournament.
They came back home with a big trophy to mark their victory.
The trophy is available for viewing at the Trophy cabinet in the Students Centre

"The mombasa tournament was a great experience for our team.
We faced tough competition but our hard work and strategy paid off.
We're proud to bring the trophy back to USIU and look forward to defending our title next year!"
- Team Captain
